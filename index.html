About Robomates Club

Robomates Club is a community of people passionate about Arduino, Robotics, Animatronics, and Programming. Our goal is to empower creators, builders, and innovators to bring their ideas to life!

Our Mission

We aim to make robotics education engaging, fun, and free.

What We Offer

Workshops, hands-on projects, guidance, and a place where you can unleash your creativity in tech and innovation.
